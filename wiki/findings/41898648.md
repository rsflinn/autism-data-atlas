# So Fragile, So Human: Noncoding DNA Regions Orchestrating Gene Expression Involved in Neurodevelopmental Disorders and in Human Brain Evolution.

## Authors


Carolina Pozzolini, Giorgia Casciaro, Martina Morales, Matheo Barone


## Abstract

The development of the human brain starts with the orchestrated expression of our genes during embryogenesis. Non-protein-coding DNA sequences (gene promoters and enhancers) dynamically interact to form a three-dimensional (3D) network, orchestrating gene expression. We discuss novel perspectives on how DNA sequence variants within regulatory DNA, identified by whole-genome sequencing (WGS), contribute to the development of neurodevelopmental disorders (NDDs), including autism spectrum disorders...
