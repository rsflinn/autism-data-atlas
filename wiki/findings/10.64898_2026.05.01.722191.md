# Pten Orchestrates Neurogenic Radial Glia Lineage Progression and Tunes Neocortical Astrocyte Production

## Key Findings
PTEN orchestrates radial glial progenitor lineage progression and influences neocortical astrocyte production in mouse cortex.

## Relevance to Atlas
PTEN is in the 49-gene set. Provides cell-type and developmental context for PTEN haploinsufficiency in autism (PTEN macrocephaly-autism syndrome).

PTEN
