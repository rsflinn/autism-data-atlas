# Learning Explainable Imaging-Genetics Associations Related to a Neurological Disorder

## Key Findings
NeuroPathX is an explainable deep learning framework using cross-attention to fuse structural MRI variations with biological pathways from genetics. Includes a sparsity loss on the attention matrix and a pathway similarity loss enforcing consistent cohort representations. Validated on autism spectrum disorder and Alzheimer's disease.

## Relevance to Atlas
Methodological advance for imaging-genetics in autism. Does not directly address noncoding regulatory variants, but the explainable attention framework could be applied to map MRI phenotypes onto gene regulatory networks in the atlas.

## Genes
None in atlas 49-gene set

## Methods
Deep learning with cross-attention, sparsity and pathway similarity losses, applied to MRI plus genetics.
