# Cerebellar acceleration of learning in an evidence-accumulation task.

## Key Findings
Cerebellum-specific autism mouse model (Purkinje cell disruption) shows accelerated learning and enhanced sensory reactivity. Links cerebellar complex-spike activity to sensory salience in autism.
