# Multi-omics profiling reveals MAGEL2-driven defects in human corticogenesis shared across Prader-Willi and Schaaf-Yang syndromes.

## Key Findings
Multi-omics profiling identifies MAGEL2 as the shared driver of corticogenesis defects across Prader-Willi and Schaaf-Yang syndromes. Schaaf-Yang has high autism penetrance.

## Relevance to Atlas
MAGEL2 not in current 49-gene set but Schaaf-Yang has substantial autism comorbidity. Multi-omics dataset on corticogenesis is methodologically relevant.

## Genes
None from 49-gene set.

## Methods
Multi-omics (transcriptomics, proteomics, epigenomics) of patient-derived neural models
