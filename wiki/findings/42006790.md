# Multiplex Portuguese Families as a Lens into rare mutations and the Shared Genetic Architecture of Schizophrenia, Mood Disorders, and Autism Spectrum Disorders.

## Key Findings
WGS of multiplex Portuguese families shows cross-diagnostic co-segregation of SZ, mood, ID and ASD. Rare large-effect variants in single neurodevelopmental genes span phenotypes.
