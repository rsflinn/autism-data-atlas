# Role of CNTN6 in neurodevelopment and neuropathology

DOI: [10.3389/fnins.2026.1726979](https://doi.org/10.3389/fnins.2026.1726979)

## Key Findings
Review of CNTN6 (contactin 6), a cell adhesion molecule expressed in cerebellum, hippocampus, and visual cortex with developmentally varying levels. Rodent studies implicate CNTN6 in neurite guidance, neural network development, and oligodendrocytogenesis. Mouse knockouts show impaired spatial orientation and memory. In humans, CNV analysis and GWAS link CNTN6 alleles to ASD, intellectual disability, Tourette syndrome, schizophrenia, anorexia, and other NDDs.
