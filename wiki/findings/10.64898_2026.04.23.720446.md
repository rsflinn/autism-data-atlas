# Linked origins but distinct roles for extreme length and sequence variation at a tandem repeat in CACNA1C

## Key Findings

Dissects a 30-bp VNTR in CACNA1C (major psychiatric GWAS locus) into length vs sequence components with distinct roles. Relevant because CACNA1 family (CACNA1A/E/G in our 49-gene set) and the broader point that VNTRs carry disease-relevant regulatory signal missed by SNP-only analyses.

## Relevance to Atlas

CACNA1C VNTR paper is not in our gene set but has direct methodological relevance. The 49-gene set includes CACNA1A, CACNA1E, and CACNA1G -- any of which could carry similar tandem repeat regulatory variation invisible to standard SNP-based WGS. Relevant to noncoding / rare structural variant workstream.
