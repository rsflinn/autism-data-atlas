# Pubertal development and hypothalamic-pituitary-gonadal axis are altered in male mice lacking Mecp2

## Key Findings

Mecp2-null male mice show disrupted puberty and HPG axis. MECP2 is in our 49-gene set (Rett). Relevance is marginal for regulatory variants but notable for MECP2 phenotype breadth.

## Relevance to Atlas

MECP2 is in the 49-gene set. This paper extends the Mecp2 phenotype beyond canonical Rett CNS features. Marginal direct relevance to regulatory variants but useful for MECP2 phenotype breadth, given our prior finding that MECP2 has prenatal-specific fetal brain eQTL signal (Werling 2020).
