# EVEE: Interpretable variant effect prediction from genomic foundation model embeddings

## Key Findings
Evo 2 (7B param genomic foundation model) embeddings achieve 0.997 AUROC on 839k ClinVar SNVs and 0.991 AUROC zero-shot on indels, outperforming existing methods. Provides interpretable disruption profiles and natural language explanations. Pre-computed predictions for all 4.2M ClinVar variants available.

## Relevance to Atlas
Potential scoring tool for Teagan's noncoding variants and VUS. The interpretable disruption profiles could help prioritize variants for functional follow-up. Complements existing tools like ChromBPNet and CADD.

Evo 2 embeddings; supervised probes; ClinVar training/validation; deep mutational scanning benchmarks; EVEE web resource.
