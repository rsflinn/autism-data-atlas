# The contribution of short tandem repeats to splicing variation in the human cortex

## Key Findings
Short tandem repeats contribute to splicing variation in the human cortex. STRs are an underexplored class of noncoding regulatory variants relevant to psychiatric and neurological disease.

## Relevance to Atlas
Directly relevant to noncoding regulatory variant architecture in autism. STRs are not captured by SNP-based GWAS or eQTL analyses; they are excluded from Teagan's GeneDx VCF.

## Methods
STR-aware genotyping; sQTL mapping in human cortex
