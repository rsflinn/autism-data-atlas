# Prefrontal Cortex 5-HT1A Receptor-Coupled Inwardly Rectifying Potassium Channels Decreased Seizure Susceptibility in Rat Models With Autism Spectrum Disorder.

## Key Findings
Rat VPA-induced ASD model shows increased seizure susceptibility with E/I imbalance in prefrontal cortex pyramidal neurons. 5-HT1A receptor activation reduces seizures through Kir3 inwardly rectifying potassium channels.

## Relevance to Atlas
Connects to ion channel disruption findings in autism-epilepsy comorbidity. Reinforces Kir3 (KCNJ family) as therapeutic target. Animal model only.

None from 49-gene set.

## Methods
VPA-induced rat ASD model; PTZ chemical kindling; whole-cell patch clamp; pharmacological 5-HT1A activation
