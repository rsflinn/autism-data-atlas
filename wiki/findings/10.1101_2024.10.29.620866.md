# Reduced dorsal CA1 Activity Limits Retention of the Temporal Component of Declarative Memory in the Cntnap2 Knockout Mouse Model of Autism

## Key Findings
Cntnap2 KO mice show reduced dorsal CA1 activity that limits temporal memory retention. Adds to evidence for hippocampal contribution to ASD cognitive phenotypes.

## Relevance to Atlas
CNTNAP2 is in the 49-gene set. Provides cell-type-specific (CA1) functional consequence of CNTNAP2 loss.

CNTNAP2

## Methods
