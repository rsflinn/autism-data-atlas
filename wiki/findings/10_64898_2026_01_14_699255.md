# Federated single-cell QTL meta-analysis reveals novel disease mechanisms

## Key Findings
Federated cis-eQTL meta-analysis across 12 studies revealing cell-type-specific genetic effects on gene expression that are obscured in bulk analyses. Identifies context-dependent regulatory mechanisms.

## Relevance to Atlas
Core methodology for the atlas: understanding how regulatory variants produce cell-type-specific effects is essential for interpreting autism noncoding variants. Could provide cell-type resolution for eQTL effects of variants near atlas genes.

Federated meta-analysis framework; single-cell RNA-seq; cis-eQTL mapping across cell types.
