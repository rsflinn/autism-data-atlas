# Autism Spectrum Disorder Caused by a Novel De Novo SCN2A Mutation: A Case Report

## Key Findings

Case report of novel de novo SCN2A frameshift in 6-year-old with ASD and language delay but no seizures. Adds to evidence that SCN2A ASD-only vs ASD+epilepsy dissociation is a real phenotypic axis.

## Relevance to Atlas

Another SCN2A ASD-without-epilepsy case. Reinforces the individual-gene-level observation from the receptor-type separation workstream: SCN2A spans both autism-only and autism+epilepsy presentations.
