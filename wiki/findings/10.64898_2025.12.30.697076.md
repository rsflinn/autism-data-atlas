# Tbx1 Heterozygosity in the Oligodendrocyte Lineage Shifts Myelinated Axon Composition in the Mouse Fimbria Without Behavioral Impairments

## Key Findings

22q11.2 / Tbx1 heterozygosity restricted to oligodendrocyte lineage shifts myelin composition but does not reproduce behavioral deficits, dissociating a cellular phenotype from behavior. Relevant to severity-disconnect workstream.

## Relevance to Atlas

Cell-lineage-restricted Tbx1 heterozygosity produces a cellular phenotype without a behavioral phenotype -- directly relevant to the severity-disruption disconnect workstream. Suggests that cell-type-specific molecular changes can dissociate from organism-level severity, consistent with our 16-metric negative severity-disruption finding.
