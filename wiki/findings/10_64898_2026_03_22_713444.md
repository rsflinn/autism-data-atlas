# NF1 expression atlas in developing mouse brain

## Key Findings
First comprehensive spatiotemporal map of NF1 expression from early embryonic stages through postnatal maturation in mouse brain. Reveals previously undocumented graded expression across brain regions and cell lineages. Integrated with snRNA-seq data for cell-type resolution.

## Relevance to Atlas
NF1 is a RAS-RAF-ERK pathway regulator implicated in neurodevelopmental disorders. The graded expression pattern may explain why specific cell populations are selectively vulnerable to NF1 loss. The cell-type expression data could inform variant interpretation for NF1 regulatory variants. NF1 is not in the core 49-gene set but is in the extended NDD gene network.

## Genes
No direct atlas genes; NF1 is in the broader NDD gene set

## Methods
In situ hybridization, immunohistochemistry, integration with snRNA-seq (adult mouse brain)
