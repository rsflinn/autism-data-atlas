# PsychENCODE Gene Regulatory Network

## Overview

A massive map of how transcription factors regulate their target genes in the human brain. 7. 35 million TF-target-enhancer links connecting transcription factors to the genes they control, with the enhancer elements that mediate the connection.

## What You Can Do With It

Look up what any transcription factor regulates in the brain. Find which TFs regulate a gene of interest. Map regulatory paths between genes. Identify shared regulatory architecture.

## Limitations

See cell-type-specific regulation (this is bulk brain). Determine developmental timing. Confirm that a predicted regulatory link is actually functional.

## Regulatory Relevance

This dataset provides regulatory annotation data that directly captures noncoding variants and regulatory elements relevant to autism genetic architecture.

## Key Genes and Coverage

FOXP1, MEF2C, TCF4, EP300, MYT1L, BCL11A
