# Epi25 Collaborative WES

## Overview

Whole exome sequencing of nearly 21,000 people with epilepsy and 33,000 controls. Tests whether specific genes carry more damaging mutations in epilepsy patients than expected by chance. Critical for autism research because many autism genes also cause epilepsy -- this dataset lets you compare which genes are loaded in epilepsy vs.

## What You Can Do With It

Look up rare variant burden for any gene in epilepsy (all epilepsy, DEE, GGE, NAFE subtypes). Compare epilepsy gene burden to autism gene convergence. Identify genes that bridge both conditions.

## Limitations

See individual-level variants (summary statistics only in browser). Study common variants (WES captures coding regions only). Directly compare to autism WES without the ASC dataset.

## Regulatory Relevance

This dataset provides regulatory annotation data that directly captures noncoding variants and regulatory elements relevant to autism genetic architecture.
